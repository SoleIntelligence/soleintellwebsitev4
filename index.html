What This Is

Medicine, policy, and the tools that actually move the needle

Sole Intelligence is the platform of Peter Sorensen, DPM, MHA — a podiatric physician and surgeon with interests in reconstructive surgery, healthcare legislation, and practical AI integration in clinical practice.

The Substack is where those threads come together: concise, field-tested perspectives on which AI tools are worth your time, how to use them in clinic and research, and why the right technology can reduce burnout without sacrificing income or outcomes.

Advocacy

Putting an MHA to work on the Hill

Dr. Sorensen has traveled to Capitol Hill to advocate for podiatric legislation, including Medicare physician fee schedule reform and the Diabetic Shoe Bill. He participated in the swearing-in ceremony of APMA's 100th President and serves on the 2025–26 APMA Emerging Leaders Program cohort.
